// GOOD QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

How do I find my current broadband speed?

Just use the live speed test at the top of this page — it measures your real download, upload and ping in a few seconds, for free. The download figure (in Mbps) is the headline number.

Why is the test slower than the speed I pay for?

Usually Wi-Fi. This test measures your device’s speed right now, so distance from the router, walls, and other devices all take their toll. Testing next to the router (or on a cable) shows your true line speed — and if there’s a big gap, better Wi-Fi is the fix.

Is slow internet the same as a slow computer?

Not always — a slow computer or weak Wi-Fi often gets blamed on the broadband. Our free health check and a quick look can tell which it is.

What if fast fibre isn’t available where I live?

In rural parts of Dorset and the New Forest, Starlink satellite internet is often the best option — we supply, install and support it.

Still buffering and slow?

Let a friendly local techie sort your broadband, Wi-Fi and devices so everything just works.
