// SECURITY AWARENESS TRAINING

Turn your team into a human firewall

Most cyber attacks start with someone clicking the wrong thing. We train your staff to spot scams and phishing, run safe practice phishing tests, and watch the dark web for your leaked passwords — friendly, jargon-free and built for small businesses. Rated 4.9 on Google.

Protect Your Team Cybersecurity
  • ● Phishing simulation
  • ● Dark-web monitoring
  • ● Plain-English, no fear

/01 — WHY IT MATTERS

Your people are the front line

The best firewall in the world won’t help if someone clicks a convincing fake email and hands over a password. The vast majority of breaches start with human error — which is exactly where a little training pays off hugely.

We make it friendly and practical (no scare tactics), so your team actually learns to pause, spot the signs and check before they click — turning your biggest risk into your best defence.

  • Bite-size, friendly training
  • Safe, simulated phishing tests
  • Reports on who needs help
  • Dark-web breach monitoring
  • Help meeting Cyber Essentials
  • Guidance for new starters
  • Real, current scam examples
  • Suits non-technical teams

// GOOD QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

What is phishing simulation?

We send your team safe, realistic fake scam emails. Anyone who clicks gets a friendly heads-up and a quick lesson — no blame, just practice that works.

Will this scare or annoy my staff?

No — we keep it friendly and supportive, not a test to catch people out. The goal is confidence, not fear.

What is dark-web monitoring?

We watch the parts of the web where stolen data is traded, and alert you if your company’s emails or passwords show up in a breach so you can change them fast.

Does this help with Cyber Essentials?

Yes — staff awareness is part of good security and supports your Cyber Essentials work.
