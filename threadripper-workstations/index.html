// GOOD QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

What is an AMD Threadripper workstation?

A workstation built around AMD’s Threadripper or Threadripper PRO processors — offering very high core counts and huge memory bandwidth for the heaviest professional workloads.

Who builds the workstations?

They’re custom-built by Scan’s award-winning 3XS Systems — building quality workstations in the UK since 1987 — and as a Scan partner we supply, set up and support them for you.

What work are they best for?

Video editing and grading, 3D rendering and animation, CAD/CAE/BIM, AI and deep learning, simulation and professional audio — anything that needs serious multicore power.

Is there a warranty?

Yes — 3XS workstations come with a 3-year parts-and-labour warranty, and we provide local setup and ongoing support on top.

Can you set it up and support it for us?

Absolutely — we configure it for your software, integrate it with your systems and support it like the rest of your IT, as your single local point of contact.
