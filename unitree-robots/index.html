/02 — QUADRUPED ROBOTS

Agile ‘robot dogs’

Go2 & Go2-W

Next-gen quadrupeds with advanced AI, real-time SLAM navigation and an open SDK. The Go2-W adds a wheel-leg hybrid drive — ideal for developers and researchers.

B2 & B2-W

Heavy-duty quadrupeds with high-performance actuators and superior mobility for industrial, commercial and research use. B2-W adds wheel-leg capability.

A2

Industrial quadruped built for rugged terrain, autonomous inspections and mission-critical operations, with modular sensors and high payload.

AS2 — coming soon

A compact industrial quadruped with enhanced payload and autonomy.

/03 — HUMANOID ROBOTS

Next-generation humanoids

R1

Compact humanoid with 3D LiDAR, AI-powered voice interaction and dual robotic arms — built for research and education.

G1

Research-focused humanoid with precise limb control, dynamic balance and an open SDK for AI development.

H1 & H1-2

Next-generation humanoids for cutting-edge research, AI development and real-world applications.

H2

Advanced humanoid emphasising mobility, intelligence and real-world adaptability for embodied AI research.

// GOOD QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

Can you really supply Unitree robots?

Yes — we’re a Scan partner, so we can supply the full Unitree range, from Go2 quadrupeds to G1 and H1 humanoids, with expert advice and ongoing support.

What’s the difference between the quadruped and humanoid robots?

Quadrupeds (‘robot dogs’ like Go2 and B2) excel at mobility, inspection and rugged terrain; humanoids (like G1 and H1) are built for research into human-like movement, manipulation and embodied AI.

Who are these robots for?

Researchers, universities, educators, industrial and security operators, and developers — anyone exploring robotics and AI.

Do you help set them up and develop on them?

Yes — we set up the robot, prepare the SDK and development environment, and provide ongoing support. It pairs naturally with our agentic AI and AI training services.

How do I get a price?

Get in touch and we’ll provide a quote through our Scan partnership for the model you need.
