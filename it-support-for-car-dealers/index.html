// FOR CAR DEALERS & FORECOURTS

IT support for car dealers & forecourts

Plain-English IT support for independent car dealers, used-car forecourts and sales showrooms across Bournemouth, Poole and Dorset — keeping your dealer-management system, stock feeds to Auto Trader, finance applications, customer records and card payments running while you sell cars. Rated 4.9 on Google.

  • ● ● Plain-English help

Selling cars today is as much a data business as a forecourt one. Your stock lives in a dealer-management system and pushes out to Auto Trader, Motors and your own website; part-exchange valuations, HPI checks and finance applications all run through web portals; and every buyer hands you a pile of personal and ID details you’re legally responsible for. When a PC dies, the broadband drops or a stock feed stops syncing, cars stop appearing online and deals stall at the desk.

We’re a family-run Dorset firm that keeps the computers, network and connection behind all of that reliable and secure — and we explain it in plain English, never jargon. We support the software you already use; we’re not a reseller trying to switch you onto something new.

// THE IT HEADACHES WE TAKE AWAY

Built around how you really work

DMS & stock feeds dropping out

Your dealer-management system is the heart of the business. We keep the PCs and connection it runs on stable, so your stock keeps syncing out to Auto Trader, Motors and your own website instead of vanishing mid-listing.

Finance & FCA-regulated data

If you broker finance you’re an FCA-authorised credit intermediary handling sensitive affordability and ID data. We help you secure the PCs and email those applications pass through, so you can meet your obligations under the FCA’s rules and Consumer Duty.

Buyer data & GDPR exposure

Driving licences, proof of address, finance and HPI details — a forecourt holds a lot of personal data. We lock down access, email and storage so you can meet your UK GDPR duties and keep customer records out of the wrong hands.

Patchy Wi-Fi across the forecourt

Sales staff need to pull up stock, valuations and finance on a tablet out on the pitch, not just at a desk. We design network and Wi-Fi that reaches the whole forecourt, showroom and prep bay reliably.

Forecourt CCTV & out-of-hours risk

Stock sits outside overnight and your camera system runs on a recorder and network that have to stay up. We keep the PCs, recorders and connection behind your CCTV working and remotely viewable.

Card & deposit payments going down

A deal can hang on taking a deposit or a card payment there and then. We keep your card machines, payment links and the network behind them reliable and secure, so you never lose a sale at the close.

// WHAT WE LOOK AFTER

Your whole IT, handled

From the everyday to the business-critical, we keep your technology working so you can get on with the job — with a real local person on the end of the phone whenever you need one.

  • Keep your dealer-management system (DMS) and stock software running on reliable, well-maintained PCs
  • Protect the stock feeds that push your cars out to Auto Trader, Motors, eBay Motors and your own website
  • Secure the email and computers your finance, HPI and part-exchange valuation portals run through
  • Lock down customer records and ID documents to help you meet UK GDPR and FCA obligations
  • Design forecourt, showroom and prep-bay Wi-Fi that reaches every corner
  • Keep card payments, deposit links and the network behind them secure and dependable
  • Set up verified backups and Microsoft 365 & email so nothing—and no deal—gets lost

// GOOD QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

Do you support our dealer-management system (DMS)?

Yes — we keep the PCs, network and internet connection your DMS, stock and CRM software run on fast and reliable. We support the system you already use; we’re not a reseller trying to move you onto a different one.

Can you keep our Auto Trader and Motors stock feeds working?

We keep the computers and connection your stock feeds depend on stable, so your cars keep syncing out to Auto Trader, Motors, eBay Motors and your own website. We look after the IT behind the feed—the advertising accounts themselves stay with you.

We arrange car finance. Can you help with FCA and data rules?

We help with the IT side. If you’re an FCA-authorised credit broker, we secure the PCs, email and storage that finance applications and ID documents pass through, so you can meet your obligations under the FCA’s rules and UK GDPR. We don’t give regulatory or legal advice—we make sure your technology supports the way you’re required to work.

Can you set up Wi-Fi that reaches the whole forecourt?

Yes — we design network and Wi-Fi coverage for the showroom, the forecourt and the prep bay, so sales staff can pull up stock, valuations and finance on a tablet anywhere on site.

Do you support Apple Macs or iPhones?

We specialise in Windows and Android, which is what nearly every dealership and forecourt runs day to day. We don’t support Apple Macs, iPhones or iPads, so we can stay genuinely expert at the systems you actually use.
