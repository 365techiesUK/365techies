/01 — WHY IT MATTERS

One wrong click shouldn’t cost you everything

Cybercrime targets homes and small businesses just as hard as big companies — because they’re often the softest targets. One dodgy email or weak password can mean locked files, a drained bank account or stolen customer data.

So we don’t rely on a single tool. We build layers of protection that work together — and we manage and monitor them for you, so security actually stays switched on instead of lapsing the moment life gets busy.

/03 — PRIVACY & VPN

Private browsing, wherever you are

Antivirus protects your device; a VPN protects your data as it travels. The two work hand in hand — so we include Malwarebytes Privacy VPN as the privacy layer of your security stack, set up and managed by us.

Safe on public Wi-Fi

Café, hotel and airport Wi-Fi is wide open. The VPN scrambles your connection so nobody on the network can snoop on what you’re doing.

Browsing kept private

Even your own broadband provider can normally see the sites you visit — with the VPN on, where you go and what you do stays private.

Bank-grade encryption

Modern WireGuard® encryption (256-bit ChaCha20) keeps everything you send scrambled and unreadable while it’s in transit.

Choose your location

Pick from 150+ servers across 60+ locations worldwide (the UK included) to mask your IP and reach your own accounts when you’re travelling.

Built-in kill switch

On Windows and Mac, if the VPN ever drops it can cut your connection rather than quietly leave you exposed.

No-logs, independently audited

A strict no-logs policy — independently audited in 2026, with no evidence of user-activity logging found.

Honest note: a VPN protects your data, not your device — it works alongside antivirus, and we’d never promise it unblocks streaming services like Netflix or BBC iPlayer.

// GOOD QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

What makes this ‘the ultimate’ security?

It’s layered. Instead of relying on one tool, we stack multiple defences — endpoint protection, email filtering, patching, multi-factor authentication, backups and monitoring — all set up, managed and watched by us. If one layer is bypassed, the next one catches it.

Do you use Malwarebytes?

Yes — as a Malwarebytes Partner we deploy Malwarebytes Premium with VPN as the endpoint layer of your security stack, set up and managed by us. See our Malwarebytes Premium & VPN page.

What does the VPN add to my security?

The VPN (Malwarebytes Privacy VPN) encrypts your internet connection so your browsing stays private — vital on public Wi-Fi, where others on the network could otherwise snoop. It also hides your location and even lets you choose which country you appear to browse from. Antivirus protects your device; the VPN protects your data as it travels — the two work hand in hand. See our Privacy VPN guide.

How do you protect against scams and viruses?

We set up endpoint protection and web filtering, keep everything patched, filter phishing email, and give you a real human to ask whether a message is safe before you click.

Do you offer multi-factor authentication?

Yes — we set up multi-factor authentication (MFA) and password managers the painless way, for home and business accounts, so a stolen password isn’t enough to get in.

Can you review our business security?

Yes — we provide plain-English business security reviews covering devices, email, backups, passwords, access and staff awareness, with clear recommendations.

I think I’ve been hacked — can you help?

Yes. Contact us straight away and we’ll help lock down your accounts, clean up affected devices, restore from backup if needed and stop it happening again.

Do you provide cyber security for small businesses in Dorset?

Yes — layered, managed protection sized for small businesses right across Dorset, Bournemouth and Poole: endpoint security, email filtering, MFA, patching, verified backups and monitoring, with a real local person to call. Pair it with Cyber Essentials certification.
