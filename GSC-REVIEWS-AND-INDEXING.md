### Tips
- Ask right after a successful job, while they're happy — that's when people say yes.
- Personalise the name + what you fixed; it lifts response a lot.
- Text usually beats email for this.
- DON'T offer anything in return for a review (against Google's rules).
- Reply to every review (even a thank-you) — it helps your local ranking and shows you care.
