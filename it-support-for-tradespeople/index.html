// FOR BUILDERS, PLUMBERS & TRADES

IT support for builders, plumbers & tradespeople

Your office is the van, and your computer is the phone in your pocket. We keep your job app, quotes, certificates and getting-paid all working — in plain English, with no jargon. Rated 4.9 on Google.

Get a Recommendation Call 01202 775566
  • ● Android phone & tablet help
  • ● Job & quoting software
  • ● Never lose your job photos

Trades run on a phone, a tablet and a laptop on the kitchen table at night. You quote on the doorstep, log jobs from the van, snap before-and-after photos as proof, raise certificates on site and chase the invoice when you get home — usually on patchy mobile signal, with cold or wet hands. When the app won’t sync, the phone won’t back up, or a quote vanishes, it’s your day’s money on the line.

We’re a local family firm and we look after the technology trades actually use: Android phones and tablets, Windows laptops and PCs, your job-management app, your accounts software and your card reader. We don’t support Apple iPhones, iPads or Macs — but most of the trade runs on Android and Windows, which is exactly our world. We help you stay on the right side of Making Tax Digital and CIS too, without pretending to be your accountant.

// THE IT HEADACHES WE TAKE AWAY

Built around how you really work

Run from the van, not a desk

Tradify, Jobber, Powered Now, YourTradebase, Commusoft, Joblogic, ServiceM8 — whatever you quote and schedule on, we keep it syncing on your phone and tablet so the job that’s in your hand is the job that’s really booked.

Lose the phone, lose the proof

Your before-and-after photos, signed job sheets and customer details live on one phone. Drop it off a roof, leave it on a site, or have it nicked from the van and it’s gone — unless it’s backed up. We set that up so it just happens.

Certificates that have to be right

Gas Safe CP12 records, NICEIC/NAPIT electrical certs, EICRs, F-Gas logs — raised on apps, emailed to customers, kept for years. We keep the device, the app and the email side reliable so your paperwork lands and is safely stored, not lost in a cracked phone.

Making Tax Digital & CIS headaches

VAT-registered trades must keep digital records and file through compatible software, and MTD for Income Tax is coming for the self-employed. Add CIS deductions on top and it’s a lot. We keep Xero, QuickBooks, FreeAgent or Sage running so YOU can meet your obligations.

Missed calls are missed jobs

You can’t answer with your hands in a fuse box, and the caller just rings the next plumber. We help with voicemail-to-email, call diverts and simple booking so enquiries don’t fall through the cracks while you’re on the tools.

Getting paid without the wait

Card readers from SumUp or Zettle, ‘pay this invoice’ links, bank-detail emails — all targets for scammers and all things that break at the worst moment. We keep your payments working and warn you about fake-invoice and supplier-bank-change fraud aimed at trades.

// WHAT WE LOOK AFTER

Your whole IT, handled

From the everyday to the business-critical, we keep your technology working so you can get on with the job — with a real local person on the end of the phone whenever you need one.

  • Keep your job-management & quoting app syncing on Android phone, tablet and laptop
  • Automatic backup of job photos, certificates and contacts so a lost phone isn’t a lost business
  • Set up and support certification apps (gas, electrical, EICR, F-Gas) and safe long-term storage
  • Keep accounts software (Xero, QuickBooks, FreeAgent, Sage) reliable for Making Tax Digital & CIS
  • Microsoft 365 and email that works the same on the phone, the tablet and the office PC
  • Card readers, payment links and invoice chasing kept working — and protected from invoice fraud
  • New Android phones, tablets and Windows laptops set up properly, with your apps and logins ready to go

// GOOD QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

Do you support my job app like Tradify, Jobber or Commusoft?

Yes — we keep the phone, tablet, laptop and internet connection your job-management app runs on fast and reliable, and help with sync, logins and printing. We support the software you already use; we’re not a reseller for any one app, so our advice stays on your side.

Can you stop me losing job photos and certificates if my phone breaks?

That’s one of the first things we sort. We set up automatic backup so your before-and-after photos, signed job sheets, contacts and certificate copies are safely saved off the phone. Lose or break the device and we get you back up and running on a new one without losing the proof you need.

Will you help me with Making Tax Digital and CIS?

We keep your accounting software (Xero, QuickBooks, FreeAgent, Sage and others) installed, updated and reliable so YOU can keep digital records and file VAT, CIS and — when it arrives — MTD for Income Tax. We’re your IT support, not your accountant: we make the software work so you and your bookkeeper can do the figures.

I only use an Android phone and a laptop — is that a problem?

Not at all — that’s exactly the kit we support. We look after Windows PCs and laptops and Android phones and tablets, which is what most of the trade runs on. Please note we don’t support Apple iPhones, iPads or Macs, so if your business runs on those we’re not the right fit — and we’ll tell you straight.

I’m on site all day — how do I actually get help?

Most things we fix remotely while you carry on working — you call us, we phone you back before we ever connect, and we sort it. When a job needs hands on the kit we come to you across Bournemouth, Poole, Christchurch and wider Dorset. Either way it’s the same friendly local faces who know how you work.

Let’s keep your tools talking

Tell us how you run your day — the app, the phone, the certs, getting paid — and we’ll keep it all working so you can stay on the tools. Pick a plan or just say hello.
