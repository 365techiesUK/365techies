// 365 AI OS

Your own AI desktop

The 365 AI OS is a real desktop that lives in your browser — sign in to your own profile and meet a built-in AI assistant that doesn’t just chat, but actually takes notes, manages your tasks and runs your desktop for you.

/01 — WHAT IT IS

A desktop that lives in your browser

The 365 AI OS is a real, working ‘operating system’ that runs in your web browser. You sign in and get your own desktop — with draggable windows, a taskbar, a Start menu and your own saved profile.

At its heart is a built-in AI assistant that does things — it takes notes, manages your to-do list, opens apps and personalises your desktop, all from a simple message. Everything you change is saved to your account and waiting for you next time.

  • Sign in to your own private profile
  • Runs in any modern browser — nothing to install
  • Notes, tasks & settings saved automatically
  • An AI assistant that takes real actions
  • Windows, taskbar & a Ctrl/⌘+K command palette
  • Built in-house by 365 Techies in Dorset

/06 — BUILT IN-HOUSE

A genuine product, not a mock-up

The 365 AI OS is something we’ve actually built — a working prototype that shows what a modern, AI-powered workspace can be: sign-in, your own saved profile, real apps, and an assistant that does the work rather than just talking about it.

When others say ‘AI OS’ they usually mean a chatbot or a rebranded web app. This is a genuine desktop you sign into, with a Claude-powered assistant that operates it — built and hosted in-house in Dorset, not aspirational branding.

It’s also a living demonstration of the custom agentic AI systems we design and build for businesses, and it pairs naturally with our AI training & adoption service. As with everything we do, it’s built with security first — in line with our cybersecurity approach — and explained in plain English.

It even includes a live off-grid power dashboard — read your Victron solar and battery system (charge, solar, loads and tank levels) from any browser, ideal for off-grid homes, campervans, motorhomes and boats.

Want a version shaped around your business — your apps, your data, your brand? Get in touch and we’ll show you what’s possible.

// GOOD QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

What is the 365 AI OS?

It’s a desktop that runs in your web browser. You sign in to your own profile and get windows, apps, a taskbar and a built-in AI assistant — everything saved to your account and ready on any device.

Is it a real product or just a concept?

It’s a real, working prototype we’ve built in-house. It also demonstrates the kind of custom agentic AI systems we build for businesses.

Do I need to install anything?

No — it runs in any modern web browser on a computer, tablet or phone. There’s nothing to download.

What can the AI assistant actually do?

It can take notes, add and complete to-do tasks, open apps and change your settings — not just answer questions. Every action is shown to you, and it only ever works on your own desktop.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. You sign in to your own private profile and your information stays yours. It’s built with security first, in line with our cybersecurity approach.

Can you build one for my business?

Absolutely — that’s the idea. We can shape a custom version around your apps, your data and your brand. Start with a discovery call.
