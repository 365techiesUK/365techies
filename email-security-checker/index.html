// THE THREE THAT STOP SPOOFING

SPF, DKIM & DMARC, in plain English

SPF

Lists which servers are allowed to send email for your domain — so random servers can’t.

DKIM

Cryptographically signs your emails, so inboxes can prove they really came from you and weren’t tampered with.

DMARC

Ties it together and tells inboxes what to do with fakes — report, spam or reject. The one most businesses are missing.

Why it matters

Without these, a scammer can email your customers or staff as ‘you’ — the root of invoice fraud and phishing.

Protects everyone

Your customers, suppliers and team all trust email from your domain — these records keep that trust safe.

Quick to fix

Setting them up is fast and behind-the-scenes — no disruption to how you send email.

// GOOD QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

What does the email security checker do?

It looks up your domain’s public DNS records — SPF, DKIM and DMARC — and tells you, in plain English, whether scammers could send email pretending to be you. It’s free, instant, and never sees your actual email.

What are SPF, DKIM and DMARC?

Three DNS records that prove your email is really from you. SPF lists who’s allowed to send as your domain, DKIM cryptographically signs your messages, and DMARC tells inboxes what to do with anything that fails — together they stop spoofing and impersonation.

Why does email spoofing matter?

Without these records, a scammer can send an email that looks exactly like it’s from your business — to your customers, staff or suppliers. It’s how invoice fraud, fake ‘CEO’ requests and phishing attacks start.

My domain isn’t fully protected — can you fix it?

Yes — setting up SPF, DKIM and DMARC correctly is something we do for businesses regularly, usually within a day and with no disruption to your email. See our cybersecurity support or get in touch.

Is it safe to enter my domain?

Completely — we only read public DNS records (the same ones every mail server checks). We don’t see your email, your inbox or any private data, and nothing is stored.

Setting up SPF, DKIM and DMARC properly stops scammers using your name — and we do it for Dorset businesses all the time, usually within a day.
