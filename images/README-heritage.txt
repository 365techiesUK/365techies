HERITAGE PHOTOS — used in the "Three decades, still on the wall" gallery on /about/
====================================================================================

Real photos of the Dorset Microsoft Education Resource Centre (Winton, Bournemouth, 1998-2008).
Owner-supplied, processed to <=1400px / optimised JPEG. The gallery (build_pages.py
heritage_gallery() + HERITAGE_IMAGES) shows only the files that exist, so you can add/remove.

In the gallery (in order):
  heritage-05.jpg  - the MERC door
  heritage-07.jpg  - inside the training centre (computers + certificate wall)
  heritage-02.jpg  - MEC Centre plaque, 2002-2003
  heritage-03.jpg  - IC3 Authorised Testing Centre plaque, 2003   (rotated upright)
  heritage-01.jpg  - Microsoft Office Specialist Testing Centre plaque, 2004   (rotated upright)
  heritage-04.jpg  - Certiport Approved Testing Centre plaque, 2006

To add more (e.g. extra training-room photos): drop a JPEG in here and add a line to
HERITAGE_IMAGES in build_pages.py, then rebuild (python build_blog.py). Orient it upright first.
