// GOOD QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

How fast can you actually help?

Most remote sessions start within minutes during opening hours (Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm). Subscribers always jump the queue, and we cover on-site visits across Bournemouth, Poole and the rest of Dorset.

Which devices do you support?

We support Windows computers and laptops, Android phones and tablets, printers, routers, smart-home devices and most consumer electronics. Please note we don’t currently offer remote support for Apple Macs, iPhones or iPads.

Is remote support safe?

Completely. Sessions run over Splashtop SOS — an encrypted, industry-standard remote support tool. You watch everything happen on your screen in real time, and access automatically ends the moment the session is over. You’re always in control.

Will you connect to my computer without warning?

No. We always phone you first to say we’re ready and to check you’re ready before we connect, and a session can only start when you click our secure link or read us a one-time code. For on-site visits we also call ahead with an estimated arrival time. We never connect out of the blue — if anyone contacts you unexpectedly demanding remote access, that’s a scam.

What happens if you can’t fix it?

We’ll always tell you straight — if a repair isn’t worth doing, we’ll say so before you spend any more. And our monthly plans are cancel-anytime with no lock-in, so you’re never paying for support you’re not happy with.

Can you support my whole business?

Absolutely. Business plans include Microsoft 365 management, network security, verified backups and on-site support across Dorset — with Dell hardware supply and IT strategy & planning when you’re ready to grow. Talk to us about a plan that fits.
