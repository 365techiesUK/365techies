    // HOW WE LOOK AFTER YOU

    What every plan includes

    • We call before we connect

      Before any remote session or full computer service, we phone you first to check you’re ready — we never connect out of the blue.

    • We call when we’re on our way

      For home and business visits we phone ahead to say we’re on our way and give you an estimated arrival time — so you know exactly when to expect us.

    • The same friendly faces

      Because we’re a family team, you deal with people who get to know you — we remember how you like your computer set up, especially as we service it every six weeks.

    // GOOD QUESTIONS

    Frequently asked

    Are these the exact prices I’ll pay?

    They’re accurate guide prices taken straight from our published plans, so what you see is what you’d expect to pay. We always confirm the exact figure with you before anything starts — no hidden call-out fees, no surprises.

    What’s included in every plan?

    Every monthly plan includes a full computer service every six weeks, remote help, regular maintenance and security checks. We also call before we connect, call ahead with an ETA for visits, and you deal with the same friendly faces. See monthly IT support.

    How is business support priced?

    Business support is priced per computer, from £24.38 per computer, per month. For larger teams we tailor a plan to how you actually work (email, hosting, users) — the calculator shows an indicative figure, then we give you a clear fixed quote.

    How much is Microsoft 365?

    Microsoft 365 is £4.85 per user, per month, added to your plan for the people who need it — and we help you choose the right licence with no upselling. Not sure which? Try our Microsoft 365 plan picker.

    Do I have to commit to a contract?

    No — every plan is monthly and cancel-anytime. There’s no long lock-in.
