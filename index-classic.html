Frequently asked

How fast can you actually help?
Most remote sessions start within 15 minutes, 24 hours a day. For on-site help, we offer same-day visits in most metro areas — just enter your ZIP at checkout to confirm availability.
Do you support both Windows and Mac?
Yes — Windows, macOS and Linux, plus iOS and Android phones and tablets, printers, routers, smart-home devices and most consumer electronics.
Is remote support safe?
Completely. Sessions run over a 256-bit encrypted connection, you watch everything happen on your screen in real time, and access automatically ends the moment the session is over. You're always in control.
What happens if you can't fix it?
For one-off jobs it's simple: no fix, no fee. Our subscription plans are cancel-anytime with no lock-in, so you're never paying for support you're not happy with.
Can you support my whole business?
Absolutely. Business 365 covers teams of up to 25 with 24/7 monitoring, a dedicated technician, same-day on-site support and an SLA. Need more seats? Talk to us about a custom plan.
