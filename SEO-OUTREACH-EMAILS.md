# 365 Techies — ready-to-send outreach emails

**How to use these**
- Find the real recipient: for press, the **newsdesk email is on each outlet's "Contact us" page** (don't guess it). For clients, send to your normal contact there.
- Replace anything in [brackets]. Sign with your own name/email.
- Send **Tue–Thu, mid-morning**. Keep press pitches short. One polite follow-up after ~5 working days is fine; no more.
- For press, **attach 1–2 good photos** (the van, the old shop, the team) — it massively raises pickup.
- Only ever claim what's true (these are written to stay accurate — e.g. we did the *wireless link*, not the fish counter; don't name the stolen-laptop customer).
