# 365 Techies — citations / directory listings (do-it-yourself, the right way)

**Why you do these (not me):** every listing needs an account + email/phone verification +
usually a CAPTCHA, all tied to your business identity. You should own and verify them yourself.

**The #1 rule: identical details everywhere (NAP consistency).** Google cross-checks your
Name, Address and Phone across the web. Inconsistency (e.g. your Yell listing saying *London*)
**hurts** local rankings. Copy-paste the master block below into every directory, unchanged.

**Quality over quantity.** Do the ~20 reputable ones below. **Avoid paid "500+ directory
submission" services** — they create spammy links that do nothing or harm you.

## Order of play
1. Tier 1 first (biggest impact), and **fix Yell**.
2. A few Tier 2 each week (don't do 20 in one day — a natural pace looks more genuine).
3. Tier 3 / local — these carry the most local-relevance weight, so don't skip them.

After ~6–8 weeks, re-check `site:365techies.co.uk` and your backlink profile — the local citations
plus the press links (see SEO-OUTREACH-EMAILS.md) are what lift the competitive rankings.
