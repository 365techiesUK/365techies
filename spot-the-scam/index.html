Frequently asked

Who is the quiz for?

Everyone — but it’s especially helpful for older or less-confident users who are targeted most. It’s friendly, quick and there’s no sign-up.

Are the examples real scams?

They’re realistic, illustrative versions of the most common scams we see — phishing emails, smishing texts, pop-ups and phone scams — so you learn the tell-tale signs safely.

Can you protect us from scams for real?

Yes — our managed cybersecurity adds layered protection, email filtering and a real human to ask ‘is this safe?’ whenever you’re unsure.
