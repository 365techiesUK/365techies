// LITHIUM POWER UPGRADES

Lithium power for vans & boats

More usable power, less weight and years more life — we design and fit Victron lithium (LiFePO4) systems for motorhomes, campervans and boats across Dorset, with solar, smart charging, inverters and monitoring. Free survey, clear quote, properly supported.

/01 — WHY LITHIUM

Power that goes the distance

Old lead-acid leisure batteries are heavy, slow to charge, and you can only safely use about half their capacity before you risk damaging them. Lithium (LiFePO4) changes all of that.

We build every system on Victron Energy — the gold standard for mobile and marine power — and set up monitoring so you can see exactly what’s going on, wherever you are.

  • Far more usable power
  • Much lighter than lead-acid
  • Faster charging from solar & engine
  • Thousands of charge cycles
  • Built on Victron Energy
  • Monitored from your phone
  • Designed around your real usage
  • Fitted & supported by us

/02 — WHAT WE FIT

A complete power system

Lithium battery banks

Right-sized LiFePO4 banks for how you actually use power.

Solar & charging

Roof solar, MPPT controllers and charging that tops up as you go.

Inverters

Run 230V appliances — from a coffee machine to power tools.

DC-DC & engine charging

Safely charge your leisure bank from the engine while you travel.

Battery monitoring

See charge, solar and usage live, on a screen or your phone (Victron VRM).

Marine-grade installs

Boat 12/24V systems built for the damp, demanding marine environment.

// GOOD QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

Do you fit lithium batteries in motorhomes and campervans?

Yes — we design and fit LiFePO4 lithium systems for motorhomes, campervans and conversions, built on Victron Energy, with solar, DC-DC charging, inverters and monitoring.

Can you upgrade a boat to lithium?

Yes — we fit marine 12/24V lithium banks, inverter/chargers and monitoring for boats around Poole Harbour and the Dorset coast, designed for the marine environment.

Why switch to lithium (LiFePO4)?

Lithium gives you far more usable power, much lighter weight, faster charging and a far longer lifespan than lead-acid — ideal for living or working off-grid in a van or on the water.

Can I monitor it from my phone?

Yes — we set up Victron monitoring so you can see your battery, solar and usage from your phone, or in our 365 AI OS off-grid dashboard.

How much does it cost?

It depends on how much power you need and what you already have — we survey it and give you a clear, fixed quote first, with no surprises.

Tell us how you travel or sail and we’ll design the right lithium system — free survey, clear quote, no obligation.
